RSV Global Dashboard

Welcome to the Global RSV Re-Emergence Dashboard. This site is dedicated to providing a comprehensive understanding of the patterns and trends of Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV) re-emergence in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Our goal is to give researchers, healthcare professionals, and policymakers the data and insights they need to effectively respond to the ongoing threat of RSV. The dashboard includes interactive data visualizations, maps, and charts that allow users to explore RSV cases and hospitalizations at the global, regional, and national levels.
